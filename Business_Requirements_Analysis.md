# Manpower Resource Provider Company - Business Requirements Analysis

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the comprehensive business requirements for a manpower resource provider company operating in India, focusing on role-based manpower deployment across multiple locations with detailed salary and compensation management.

---

## 1. Business Overview

### 1.1 Company Profile
- **Business Type**: Manpower Resource Provider / Staffing Solutions
- **Geography**: Pan-India Operations
- **Service Model**: B2B (Business to Business)
- **Core Service**: Role-based manpower deployment with comprehensive compensation management

### 1.2 Value Proposition
- Flexible manpower solutions across multiple locations
- Transparent salary breakup and cost structure
- Compliance with state-specific labor laws

## 3. Company Service Components

### 3.1 Compensation Components Provided

#### A. Standard Allowances
1. **DA (Dearness Allowance)**
   - Percentage of Basic Salary
   - Varies by location (metro/non-metro)
   - Inflation-indexed component
   - Typical Range: 15-30% of Basic

2. **HRA (House Rent Allowance)**
   - Based on city classification
   - Metro cities: 50% of Basic
   - Non-metro: 40% of Basic
   - Tier-2/3 cities: 30% of Basic

3. **Conveyance Allowance**
   - Fixed amount per employee
   - Varies by role and location
   - Typically ₹1,600 - ₹3,200 per month

4. **Special Allowance**
   - Balancing component to reach CTC
   - Fully taxable
   - Flexible component

#### B. Statutory Components
1. **Provident Fund (PF)**
   - Employee contribution: 12% of Basic + DA
   - Employer contribution: 12% of Basic + DA
   - Mandatory for establishments with 20+ employees

2. **Employee State Insurance (ESI)**
   - Employee contribution: 0.75% of gross salary
   - Employer contribution: 3.25% of gross salary
   - Applicable for salary up to ₹21,000/month

3. **Gratuity**
   - 4.81% of Basic salary (annual provision)
   - Payable after 5 years of service

4. **Professional Tax**
   - State-specific (varies by state)
   - Maximum ₹2,500 per annum

#### C. Additional Benefits
1. **Bonus**
   - Statutory bonus: 8.33% of salary (minimum wage basis)
   - Performance bonus: As per agreement

2. **Leave Encashment**
   - Earned leave provision
   - Typically 1.25 days per month

3. **Medical Insurance**
   - Group mediclaim policy
   - Coverage amount as per agreement

4. **Uniform & Safety Equipment**
   - For specific roles (security, housekeeping, technical)
   - Annual provision

## 7. Business Process Flow

### 7.1 Customer Onboarding Process

**Step 1: Requirement Gathering**
- Customer submits requirement form
- Details captured:
  - Roles required
  - Number of resources per role
  - Location details (State, City, Site)
  - Expected start date
  - Contract duration
  - Budget/salary expectations

**Step 2: Requirement Analysis**
- Validate against minimum wage compliance
- Check location-specific regulations
- Assess resource availability
- Calculate cost structure

**Step 3: Proposal Generation**
- Create detailed salary breakup per role
- Include all statutory components
- Add company margin/service charges
- Present location-wise cost summary

**Step 4: Negotiation & Finalization**
- Discuss and finalize terms
- Sign Master Service Agreement (MSA)
- Define SLAs and KPIs
- Establish payment terms

**Step 5: Resource Deployment**
- Source and screen candidates
- Conduct interviews and verification
- Complete documentation
- Deploy resources to customer location

**Step 6: Ongoing Management**
- Monthly attendance tracking
- Salary processing
- Statutory compliance
- Performance monitoring
- Replacement management

## 8. Key Business Considerations

### 8.1 Compliance Requirements

#### A. Labor Law Compliance
- Contract Labor Act registration
- Shops & Establishment Act
- Minimum Wages Act
- Payment of Wages Act
- Payment of Bonus Act
- Employees' Provident Fund Act
- ESI Act

#### B. Documentation Requirements
- Employee contracts
- Attendance records
- Wage registers
- PF/ESI returns
- Form 16/Form 12BA
- Leave records
- Termination/resignation documents

### 8.2 Risk Management

#### A. Operational Risks
- **Resource Attrition**: Maintain buffer pool of 10-15%
- **Quality Issues**: Regular training and monitoring
- **Compliance Violations**: Periodic audits
- **Payment Delays**: Credit terms and advance payments

#### B. Financial Risks
- **Margin Pressure**: Optimize cost structure
- **Bad Debts**: Credit evaluation and insurance
- **Statutory Changes**: Regular policy updates
- **Currency Fluctuations**: Fixed-term contracts

### 8.3 Pricing Strategy

#### A. Cost Components
1. **Direct Costs**
   - Employee salaries (Basic + Allowances)
   - Statutory contributions (PF, ESI, Gratuity)
   - Bonus and leave provisions

2. **Indirect Costs**
   - Recruitment costs
   - Training expenses
   - Administrative overhead
   - Technology and systems
   - Insurance premiums

3. **Service Margin**
   - Typically 8-15% of total cost
   - Varies by volume and contract duration
   - Higher for specialized roles

#### B. Pricing Models
1. **Per Employee Per Month (PEPM)**
   - Fixed monthly charge per resource
   - Includes all costs and margin

2. **Cost Plus Model**
   - Actual costs + fixed percentage margin
   - Transparent pricing

3. **Volume-based Discounts**
   - Tiered pricing for large deployments
   - Long-term contract benefits

## 11. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 11.1 Operational KPIs
- **Deployment Time**: Average time from requirement to deployment
- **Attrition Rate**: Monthly/Annual turnover percentage
- **Attendance Rate**: Average attendance across all resources
- **Replacement Time**: Time taken to replace absent/resigned employees
- **Training Completion**: Percentage of employees trained

### 11.2 Financial KPIs
- **Revenue per Employee**: Monthly revenue generated per deployed resource
- **Gross Margin**: Percentage margin on services
- **Collection Efficiency**: Percentage of invoices collected on time
- **Cost per Hire**: Average recruitment cost per employee
- **Payroll Accuracy**: Percentage of error-free salary processing

### 11.3 Compliance KPIs
- **Statutory Filing**: On-time PF/ESI return filing
- **Documentation Completion**: Percentage of complete employee files
- **Audit Findings**: Number of compliance issues identified
- **License Renewals**: Timely renewal of all registrations

### 11.4 Customer Satisfaction KPIs
- **Customer Retention Rate**: Percentage of renewed contracts
- **NPS Score**: Net Promoter Score
- **Complaint Resolution Time**: Average time to resolve issues
- **SLA Adherence**: Percentage of SLA targets met
